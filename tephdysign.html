About

TephdySign is the second newest website built by aspiring website and graphic designer Joseph Amandy. The creator spent almost six months building this minimalist website design that will showcase his creativity in terms of graphics art. TephdySign will be a live witness of his growth and improvement as a graphic artist and website designer as well. This might be a static website for now but as times goes by and he gains more website designing skills, this surely will transform from static to dynamic website with more interaction to its user (visitor). The contents will be also become more eye-catching soon.

TephdySign came from mixing the two words Tephdy and Design. Tephdy is his mispronunciation of his name Joseph Amandy. Design came from literally design itself, because Joseph Amandy is really has a passion in designing and on how to improve it.

All that passion in website and graphic designing are the reason of TephdySign existence.
